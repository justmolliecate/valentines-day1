Optimistic Melancholy

close my eyes,
dream of you.

nights and days,
together.
hands, lips, bodies
together.
hearts, souls, minds
together.

open my eyes,
live my life.

breathing different
air.
walking different
streets.
seeing different
worlds.
the slow melancholy of 5,000 miles.

but then,
somewhere in the in-between,
the sweetest time of dusky light,
the change of seasons passing by,
my dreams of you
become the most
beautiful
and insane
reality.

- Mollie Lemm

written for you, my love
